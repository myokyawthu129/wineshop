About Us

We are a multi award-winning fine wine merchant based in the heart of Lancashire’s Ribble
Valley. We aspire to be part of a ‘new generation’ of wine merchants, making the act of
buying fine wine and spirits both more accessible and more enjoyable.

​​​​​​​ Come down to the shop and see our expert staff in-store, grab a glass of wine from our
tasting menu, and view our full range of Fine Wines, Champagnes, Premium Spirits, Craft
Beers, and Cigars!

Our shop

, at the corner of King Street and Church Lane, has long
been used as a wine shop, however its previous incarnation was as part
of the national chain ‘Threshers’. Tom, having graduated with a recent
law degree was running this shop for the chain at the time.  When
Threshers was forced into administration in December 2009 the shop
closed its doors.
Fast forward to April 2010 and Tom at just 24 had taken over the shop
as a family owned fully independent wine merchants. On the 20th of
April 2010 The Forward Wine Shop began trading for the first time.
The aim from the start was clear: to find superb quality wines that really over delivered at the price; and to sell these wines with above all fantastic customer service.

Our People

The team of people who make up The Forward Wine Shop are the most vital part of it. When I set the business up the aim for me was always clear, to have great people who were passionate about wine on hand to give great advice to our customers to make sure they got excellent service and exactly the wine they came in for.
This has never changed. The passion for wine is the key element, all the other skills that people bring with them or learn on the job make them great employees for any business but what makes them great for The Forward Wine Shop is that love and passion for wine and the desire to convey that to our customers.
